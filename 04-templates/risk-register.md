## Field definitions

1. **Type.** Obstacle (risk) or Opportunity. Same discipline, both directions.
2. **Statement.** Obstacles: event, caused by, resulting in. Opportunities: event or condition, enabling, resulting in. Vague statements produce vague treatments.
3. **Scores.** From the organization's calibrated scales only; the scale document is versioned configuration, referenced here, never improvised.
4. **Residual.** Never better than inherent without the cited controls; the CTL-ids are the explanation.
5. **Appetite position.** Above, At or Within, from A1 statements. Above with no treatment decision is the standing exception report.
6. **Treatment decision.** Risks: reduce, avoid, transfer, accept. Opportunities: pursue, enhance, share, decline. **Decided by / date** makes acceptance a named act.
7. **Review cadence.** Tiered by rating: high quarterly, medium semiannual, low annual.

## Guidance

1. Objective linkage is mandatory at entry; a category-only register drifts generic.
2. Track rating history; trajectory tells you more than position.
3. Watch for assessment inflation and deflation across units; calibrate assessors, not just scales.
